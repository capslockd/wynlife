A Church Where
Everyone Belongs

WynLife Church is a vibrant, multicultural community in the Wyndham area of Melbourne's west. We gather every Sunday to worship, hear from God's Word, and grow together in faith.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church to call home, there's a place for you here.

Children's Ministry

Sunday School

WynLife runs a Sunday School program for children during the Sunday service, built on the Generations of Grace curriculum — teaching children God's redemptive plan through Bible narrative, from Genesis to Revelation.

Every lesson is age-appropriate, engaging, and rooted firmly in the truth of God's Word. It's a place where kids can learn, ask questions, and grow in their faith in a fun and welcoming environment.
