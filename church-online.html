What to Expect

Our Sunday Services

Our Sunday gatherings are a friendly time of worship for all ages and diverse cultural backgrounds — warm, welcoming, and not too formal.

🎵 Contemporary Worship
Our capable worship team leads us in dynamic, heartfelt praise each Sunday.
📖 Biblical Teaching
Expository preaching grounded in Scripture, relevant for everyday life.
👪 All Ages Welcome
Children's program runs during the service. Everyone is included.
☕ Community Time
Stay after the service for morning tea and connection with the community.

A vibrant, multicultural Christian community in Melbourne's west — committed to knowing Jesus, growing in faith, and serving our community.
