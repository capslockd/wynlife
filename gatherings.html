Children's Ministry

Sunday School

WynLife runs a Sunday School program for children during the Sunday service, built on the Generations of Grace curriculum — a Christ-centred Bible narrative program that teaches children God's redemptive plan from Genesis to Revelation.

Generations of Grace Curriculum

The Generations of Grace Sunday school curriculum teaches God's redemptive plan through Bible narrative. Children learn the grand story of Scripture — who God is, what He has done, and how Jesus is at the centre of it all.

Each lesson is designed to be age-appropriate, engaging, and rooted in the truth of God's Word. Parents can feel confident their children are receiving solid Biblical teaching week after week.

Learn About the Curriculum
